What AI-Ready Means at ATO

🧠

Think With AI

Use AI as a thinking partner. Recognize when to delegate tasks to models and how to stay in control.

✍️

Write Effective Prompts

Craft clear, specific prompts that get you the results you need. Master the anatomy of a good request.

⚙️

Apply AI to Real Work

Integrate AI into your actual role — email, research, planning, communication, data work, and more.

🔒

Handle Privacy & Clients

Navigate confidentiality and client communication with confidence. Know what's safe to share with AI.

📚

Learn Independently

Own your growth. Use AI as a learning tool and continue building your skills beyond the program.

✅

Prove It

Demonstrate competency through a capstone project that shows how you'd use AI in your day-to-day work.

The 5 Sessions

One live alignment session to kick things off. Four self-paced sessions you can work through at your own pace.

01
Live Session 45-60 min

Kickoff

The AI Landscape, what tools exist, what they're good at, and the smart intern mental model. Alignment on expectations, goals, privacy guidelines, and client communication.

What you'll cover:

  • The AI landscape and available tools
  • Capabilities and limitations of each tool
  • The "smart intern" mental model
  • Expectations and program goals
  • Privacy and client communication guidelines

Outcome: Alignment and foundation for the program.

View Full Session →
02
Self-Paced ~45 min

Prompting Foundations

Master the anatomy of a good prompt: role, context, task, format, and constraints. Learn iteration and see before/after examples from real work.

What you'll cover:

  • Anatomy of a good prompt (role, context, task, format, constraints)
  • Before/after examples from real work
  • The "be specific" principle
  • How to iterate and improve prompts

Exercise: Complete 3 tasks with prompts to practice your skills.

View Full Session →
03
Self-Paced ~30 min

Getting Comfortable With Free Tools

Try at least 2 free tools, run the same prompt in both, and compare. Explore use cases and form your own preference.

What you'll cover:

  • Overview of free AI tools available
  • How to evaluate tool quality
  • Use cases for each tool
  • Practical comparison techniques

Exercise: Complete a task in 2 different tools and write a comparison note.

View Full Session →
04
Self-Paced ~1 hour

AI for Real Work Tasks

Apply AI across 7 core areas: email triage, research, task planning, communication, data work, editing, and client communication.

7 Core Areas:

  • Email triage and response
  • Research and synthesis
  • Task planning and prioritization
  • Communication drafting
  • Data work and analysis
  • Editing and proofreading
  • Client communication

Exercise: Complete 3 or more tasks with AI across different areas from your own work.

View Full Session →
05
Capstone Output Required

Capstone + Competency Check

Demonstrate how you'd use AI in your role through 3-5 tasks. Show what you learned and reflect on client communication.

Capstone Components:

  • 3-5 tasks showing how you'd use AI in your role
  • Sample deliverables demonstrating competency
  • Reflection on what you learned
  • Client communication reflection
  • Brief review with your point person

Outcome: Verified competency and personalized AI strategy for your role.
