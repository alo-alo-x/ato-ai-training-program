Frequently Asked Questions

How long does this take?

About 10-15 hours spread over two weeks. The sessions are self-paced, so you can fit them around your schedule.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. If you can send a text message, you can use these tools. The program starts from zero.

What tools do I need?

Just a computer and internet access. We'll use free AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) during training. Paid tools get set up after you pass.

What if I get stuck?

Your point person (Alli or Jas) is available throughout. There's no penalty for asking questions.

What happens after I pass?

You get upgraded to paid AI tools (Claude Pro + Cowork), start working with your client, and join our continuing education program.

Talk to your point person to schedule your kickoff, or jump right in if you're already set up.
