Session 1: Kickoff

Your first step into AI at ATO

Live Session 45–60 minutes

Welcome to AI Training

This is a hands-on program that will make you confident using AI in your day-to-day work. Session 1 is a live session where we'll get aligned on what AI is, what it can do, and how we use it at ATO.

What You'll Learn

  • What AI tools are available (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot)
  • What AI is good at, and where it falls short
  • The "smart intern" mental model for working with AI
  • How this training program works and what's expected of you
  • Privacy and client guidelines. what's OK and what's not

Before the Session

  • Sign up for at least one free AI tool. Try claude.ai, chatgpt.com, or gemini.google.com
  • Think of one task from your work that feels repetitive or time-consuming
  • Come with questions

During the Session

What to Pay Attention To

  • Live demo. The facilitator will show the same prompt in two different tools. Watch for how the results differ.
  • Privacy guidelines. These matter for every role, not just client-facing ones.
  • Program timeline. Understand what's coming in Sessions 2 through 5.

After the Session

What to Do Next

  • Reflect. What's one thing from this session that surprised you?
  • Save your task idea. You brought a work task with you. You'll use it starting in Session 2.
  • Move on to Session 2 when you're ready. Sessions 2 onwards are self-paced. Go at your own speed.

FAQ

Do I need to be technical?

No. This program is for everyone.

What if I've already used AI?

Great. You'll refine your skills and learn ATO's approach.

Can I use AI for personal stuff too?

Yes, but this program focuses on work applications.

What happens if I fall behind?

It's self-paced after this session. Go at your own speed.
